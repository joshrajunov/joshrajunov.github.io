Meet Josh Rajunov, a sophomore Broadcast Digitial Journalism student at the Syracuse University Newhouse School of Public Communications. Josh is native to Dallas, Texas, where he was born and raised. He is a huge fan of all Dallas Sports, especially the Texas Rangers.

Josh is an aspiring sports broadcaster with an interest in play-by-play commentary. This summer, Josh will be the play-by-play commentator for the Sherman Shadowcats, an independent baseball team in the Mid America League. In the past and still active, he has been a commentator for the NFHS Network, NEC Front Row Network, and the Onondaga Lazers Network. Josh Also works for CitrusTV, WAER 88.3 FM, and WJPZ Z89.
